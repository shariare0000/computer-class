MARVEL

Marvel refers to the multimedia franchise of comic books, movies, and television shows produced by Marvel Entertainment and its subsidiaries, primarily Marvel Comics and Marvel Studios. The franchise is known for its extensive roster of superheroes and villains, many of whom inhabit a shared universe known as the Marvel Universe.

deadpool

Deadpool, whose real name is Wade Wilson, is a Marvel Comics antihero known for his humor, violent antics, and unique self-awareness. The character routinely breaks the fourth wall by talking directly to the audience, and he is a popular fixture in comic books, movies, and video games

Spider-Man

Spider-Man is a fictional superhero who appears in Marvel Comics and various films, television shows, and video games. The character is famously the alter ego of Peter Parker, a young man who gains superhuman abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. Guided by the motto "With great power comes great responsibility," he uses his powers to protect New York City as a costumed hero

Iron Man

Iron Man refers to the Marvel Comics superhero alter-ego of billionaire industrialist Tony Stark. Outside of the comics, the character is most famously known for his portrayal by actor Robert Downey Jr. in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) films
