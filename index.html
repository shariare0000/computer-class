farming Guides

Comprehensive farming guides cover the entire process, from initial business planning and soil preparation to harvest, marketing, and record-keeping. The right approach depends on whether you're interested in traditional, organic, sustainable, or specialized techniques like vertical farming.

Crop details

Crop details vary by region and purpose, but generally fall into categories like food crops (cereals, pulses, vegetables, fruits), beverage crops (tea, coffee), and cash crops. Major world food crops include rice, wheat, and maize, while specific details for a location like Bangladesh show rice as the most important crop, followed by wheat, maize, and various oilseeds and pulses.

Pest contorl

Pest control can be approached through prevention, identification, and control/elimination, or by using methods like hygiene, biological, and mechanical solutions. Prevention involves sealing entry points and removing food and water sources, while control methods include chemical treatments, physical traps, or using natural predators. A key first step is correctly identifying the pest to choose the most effective treatment.
